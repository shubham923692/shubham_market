
Shubham Market — Free Static Marketplace (Phone-only deploy)
====================================================

Quick Launch (Netlify Drop):
1) Download the ZIP.
2) Open https://app.netlify.com/drop on your phone.
3) Upload the ZIP → you get an instant live URL.
4) Share the URL. Done.

Edit from phone:
- Change WhatsApp number in app.js (ADMIN_WHATSAPP).
- Add or edit listings in data/listings.json (copy an object and change fields).
- Re-zip and re-upload to Netlify Drop to update.

Policy:
- We do NOT facilitate sale of accounts where platform Terms prohibit it.
- List only compliant assets/services and handle handover responsibly.
